WHAT MAKES OUR SCHOOL SO SPECIAL?

  • You and your family will easily adapt and feel warmly welcomed in this truly and multicultural school with more than 5 different nationalities represented in our student and staff population.
  • Your children will learn safely in our No Bully Zone.
  • Due to our double accreditation with the Council of Schools (CIS) and the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC), our school is able to offer our Seniors the possibility of graduating with TWO Diplomas: The Baccalaureate Diploma, and Colombian High School Diploma.
  • We are among the schools in Colombia that consider English as the first language of instruction for your children, and the curriculum is taught by a high percentage of internationally qualified teachers who speak English as a first language.
  • We offer an curriculum recognised worldwide. Spanish and Colombian social studies are offered at different levels catering for the needs of our students.
  • We offer you innovative and diverse programmes for both Co-Curricular Activities and Performing Arts that take place during the school day.

Welcome to IE TAPARTO

A community of dynamic and global learners where the heart of our educational experience is focussed on the development of the whole child. We deliver a broad and balanced curriculum that offers well-known and respected academic programmes, as well as opportunities for personal growth and enrichment. Our founding principles, beliefs and mission are underpinned and lived everyday through the eight foundation blocks.

IE TAPARTO offers small class sizes, highly qualified teachers and a broad specialized support team to ensure that students are treated as individuals and receive the orientation that each one needs to succeed academically and flourish as a person. Our “No Bully Zone” ensures a caring and safe environment for students to learn, explore and make friends.

Filomena Maya
Director
